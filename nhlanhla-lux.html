Nhlanhla Lux Biography

Nhlanhla Lux is a South African businessman, activist, and motivational speaker. Mr. Lux is also the founder of Native Airways and a pilot.

He first caught public attention when he and his teammates saved Maponya Mall from unknown looters. By holding the mall, he becomes a celebrity in the eyes of Africans. He is then appointed as the commander of Operation Dudula. He has often been described as a man of kindness and honor.

Furthermore, he frequently speaks up for the oppressed, distinguishing him as a true leader. He is constantly involved in various legal matters in Soth Africa.

In terms of education, Nhlanhla Lux started at Parkhurst Primary School and then transferred to Jeppe High School for Boys. However, he later completed his high school education at St David’s Marist after receiving scholarship offers from them due to his outstanding athletic abilities.
