Stacey Abrams husband: Is Stacey Abrams married?

Stacey Yvonne Abrams was born on December 9, 1973. Abrams is the national face of the voting rights movement.

She founded and inspired a number of groups dedicated to voter registration and mobilization, particularly among young people and people of color.

Because she was the first lady and the first African American woman to occupy roles in state and national politics, Stacey Abrams’ name has become synonymous with voting access and turnout.

In February 2021, she was nominated for the Nobel Peace Prize.

Abrams has won several awards for her efforts as a public servant, including the John F. Kennedy New Frontier Award (2012) and the Georgia AFL-Friend CIO’s of Labor Award.
