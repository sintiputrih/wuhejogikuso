Mark Zuckerberg Training with UFC Champions Israel Adesanya &amp; Alexander Volkanovski Before Potential


Training alongside billionaire Facebook founder Mark Zuckerberg and UFC featherweight champion, Alexander Volkanovski, UFC middleweight champion Israel Adesanya shared photos on Tuesday night, July 11.

Sharing the photos on Twitter, Adesanya captioned them, “No fugazi with Mark, This is serious Business.”

These photos emerge amidst the possibility of a fight between Zuckerberg and his rival, Elon Musk, as their social media platforms clash for supremacy.
