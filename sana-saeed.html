Sana Saeed is an Indian actress and model. She has appeared in several Indian TV serials and films. She is well-known for her role as Anjali in the movie Kuch Kuch Hota Hai.

Wiki/Biography

Sana Saeed was born on Thursday, 22 September 1988 (age 33 years; as of 2021) in Mumbai. Her zodiac sign is Virgo. She holds a bachelor’s degree in Business and Management Studies from H. R. College of Commerce and Economics, Mumbai. She also holds a diploma and an advanced diploma in Marketing and Advertising. In 2016, she did a certificate course in acting and filmmaking at the New York Film Academy, Los Angeles.
