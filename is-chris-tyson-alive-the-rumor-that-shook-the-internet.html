Is Chris Tyson Alive? The Rumor That Shook the Internet

Chris Tyson, also known as Lil Tjay’s friend, is a social media star who frequently appears in MrBeast’s videos. He is also a transgender activist who started hormone replacement therapy in 2022. But in April 2023, a rumor spread online that Chris Tyson had died, causing panic and confusion among his fans. Was the rumor true, or was it a hoax? Here is what we know so far.

The Origin of the Rumor

The rumor that Chris Tyson had died started on April 17, 2023, when a website called Channel 22 News published an article with the headline: “Social Media Star Chris Tyson Found Dead in His Los Angeles Home”. 1 The article claimed that Chris Tyson had died from a heart attack and that his body was discovered by his wife. The article also included a photo of Chris Tyson and a quote from his agent.

However, the article was completely fake and had no basis in reality. Channel 22 News is a prank website that allows users to create and share fake news stories with their friends. The website has a disclaimer at the bottom that reads: “You clicked this fake news story. Now make your own!”

The Spread of the Rumor

Despite being clearly fake, the article quickly went viral on social media, especially on Facebook and Twitter. Many fans of Chris Tyson were shocked and saddened by the news, while others were skeptical and questioned its authenticity. Some users also shared screenshots of the article, which made it look more credible.

The rumor was further fueled by another prank website called Breaking News 365, which published a similar article with the headline: “Chris Tyson Shot Dead in Los Angeles”. 2 The article repeated the same false information as the previous one, but added a different cause of death. The article also included a photo of Chris Tyson and a quote from his friend.

Breaking News 365 is another prank website that allows users to create and share fake news stories. The website has a disclaimer at the bottom that reads: “This is a joke website that is intended for fun. You can create your own fake news and trick your friends by sharing it on social networks.”

The Response of Chris Tyson

Chris Tyson himself was aware of the rumor and responded to it on his social media accounts. On April 18, 2023, he posted a video of him smiling and waving to the camera, with the caption: “I’m alive and well y’all. Don’t believe everything you read on the internet. Love you all.” 3 He also thanked his fans for their support and prayers, and joked that he was working on a new project called “Chris 4 Life”.

Chris Tyson also addressed the rumor in an interview with Popular Bio on April 19, 2023. He said: “It was crazy. I mean, it’s not funny that people actually believed it, but it’s funny that somebody would actually sit there and make it.” 4 He also said that he was not bothered by the rumor and that he was used to dealing with trolls and haters online. He also said that he was recovering well and that he was grateful to be alive.

The Conclusion

Chris Tyson is not dead. He is alive and well and continuing his successful social media career. The rumor was a result of two prank websites that allowed users to create and share fake news stories. The rumor was spread by social media users who either believed it or wanted to prank their friends. Chris Tyson himself responded to the rumor with humor and positivity, and did not let it affect him negatively.
