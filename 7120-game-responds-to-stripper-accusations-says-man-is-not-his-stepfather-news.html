Game Responds To Stripper Accusations, Says Man Is Not His Stepfather

For years there has been speculation as to whether or not gangster rapper Game was indeed a male stripper, and did indeed have a tongue ring for a period of time. Now these rumors have started to re-circulate, due to his apparent stepfather, Hodari Sababu.

Sababu appeared in the documentary "Hood Life:  A Hip Hop Guided Tour of Compton," as we reported yesterday, and confirmed Game's short time as a male stripper. Game has not taken lightly to these accusations, going on Twitter to deny them.

Game tweeted angrily, claiming that the Sababu is lying, "This fag get out of jail & because I dont give em no money... He do a interview with Shade 45 lying wit the same stripper story.. ha ha..."

He continued, "It don't work nigga. It's old, niggas tried dat 1 & niggas still thinkin that bullshit gone END me ? I'm to the Canyons on yo bum ass nigga."

Finally he said Sababu isn't even his stepfather, "Hol up.... Talkin bout he my stepfather ? Bitch, my mama aint never been MARRIED !!! Mad cause I aint loan yo gay ass no $$$#FuckOuttaHere."
