Travis Scott Gets &quot;Tour Ready&quot; With New Head Tattoo

New York, NY - 

Before Travis Scott hits the road on the recently announced Astroworld: Wish You Were Here Tour, he decided to treat himself to some new ink.

On Thursday (August 16), La Flame shared a photo of New York-based tattoo artist Scott Campbell going to work on his scalp.

“Getting tour ready,” he wrote in the caption.

The black tattoo appears to be some kind of tribal design on the left side of his head, although Scott has yet to explain its significance.
