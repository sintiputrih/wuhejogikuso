LeAnn Rimes Net Worth

LeAnn Rimes:

$20 million

LeAnn Rimes is an American singer. She was only 13 when she came to prominence courtesy of the song “Blue”. This cemented her career as a country star. In 1996, she released her first album also called Blue. Her music later had a variation of pop when she released her second album You Light Up My Life: Inspirational Songs. She released two more albums in the ’90s: Sittin’ on Top of the World and LeAnn Rimes. Her fifth album came with 2002’s Twisted Angel. She released four more albums in the 2000s. She’s also ventured into acting with guest appearances in American Dreams, Drop Dead Diva, and Anger Management.
