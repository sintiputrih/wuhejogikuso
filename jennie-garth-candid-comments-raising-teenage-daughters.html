Jennie Garth speaks candidly about parenting her children with ex Peter Facinelli

The former couple share three daughters

    The star spoke to about her girls – Luca, 23, Lola, 18, and 14-year-old Fiona – and admitted that raising teenagers is a "whole different ball game".
