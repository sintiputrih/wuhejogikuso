Veep, Silicon Valley, and The People v. O.J. Simpson Lead the Directors Guild of America TV Nominati

The nominations are in for Outstanding Directorial Achievement in TV, Commercials, and Documentary Films for the 69th annual Directors Guild of America Awards. HBO and FX lead the nominations in narrative series programming, with Veep, The People v. O.J. Simpson, Westworld, Game of Thrones, Silicon Valley, and Atlanta filling up nine of the ten available slots. Netflix’s Stranger Things was the only non-FX or HBO show to break into the club, with the Duffer Brothers getting nominated for the episode “Chapter One: The Vanishing of Will Byers.” The feature film nominations for the DGA awards will be announced tomorrow.
