Meet MAFS Nate Barnes! His Bio, Height, Parents, Job, Age

Nate Barnes was ambitious and wanted a partner to match his ambitious mindset. So, what better place than Married At First Sight to find someone with resonates with his energy? He joined the successful matchmaking series for its 15th season in 2022.

Keep reading this Nate Barnes Bio to learn more about him.

Married At First Sight (MAFS): Are Nate Barnes And Stacia Kracher Still Together?

It’s unclear if Nate Barnes and Stacia Kracher are still together. But from what we’ve seen, the duo had the best chance out the gate! The couple was a good fit based on key factors like ambitions and past upbringing.

As per Nate, he was “looking forward” to finding a lifelong travel buddy to whom he was “mentally, spiritually, and physically attracted to.” And although he did admit that finding a perfect partner is “intentional high-risk, high reward,” his mindset led him to trust the experts to do the job for him.

The experts, that season, were Pastor Cal, Dr. Pepper, DeVon Franklin, and Dr. Pia Holec.

Our star further added that he signed up for MAFS because he wanted a “best friend, partner, and somebody to explore the whole world with.” But when waiting at the altar, realizing he was in an experimental show, he thought to himself, “What the hell did I sign up for?”

Luckily, the beautiful Stacia was able to calm him down. Just one look at Stacia, Nate was instantly charmed. The two then exchanged vows and proceeded to sign a prenup, Stacia even asked Nate to sign a postnup after asking about his credit score. This might be a turn-off to some. But Nate was already diving head first into the experiment.

Talking about Stacia, she might have joined MAFS for a different reason, but her ultimate goal overlapped with that of Nate. While Nate was hoping to find a lifelong travel buddy Stacia was looking for a man that meets her “high standards.”

“I refuse to settle. I’d rather be single than settle just to say I’m in a relationship,” she explained. Also, the charmer added the “most important thing” she was looking for in a relationship was “trust” —  “I want to know wholeheartedly that I can trust my partner with anything. He is a safe place for me to land, to be vulnerable, and that I can trust his intentions,” she added.

Joining Nate and Stacia for season 15 of MAFS were their co-stars — Lindy and Miguel, Krysten and Mitch, Alexis and Justin, and Morgan and Binh.

Nate Barnes Job

Nate Barnes is a day trader. He founded Bullish Prosperity for new traders who wanted to learn how to trade forex. “Bullish Prosperity was created to shorten the learning curve so you can focus on the big picture. We break down MetaTrader 4, trading view, and how to trade currencies with trusted strategies that can help you long-term as a day trader,” he explained.

However, his 1 on 1 mentorship program was quite expensive — around $1500 (discounted price) for 2 weeks. His program was 100% live and held on Zoom calls for 1 hour a day. 

So, how did Nate start forex trading? Well, it was 2017 when Nate was doing marketing and adverting for lawyers in a small marketing agency when he stumbled upon “this group of people who would come super late, at around like 10 at night.” He was then intrigued by what they did and turns out they were forex traders. And it wasn’t long before he joined them.

Now, Nate has thousands of followers, and his popularity even led him to start merch. The last we checked he offered caps for $21.25 and Tess for $18.70.
