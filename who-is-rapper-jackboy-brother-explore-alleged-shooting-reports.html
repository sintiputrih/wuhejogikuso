Who Is Rapper Jackboy Brother? Explore Alleged Shooting Reports

Haitian rapper Jackboy’s brother has been reportedly shot and is said to be in critical condition. In the wake of all the rumors, people wanted to know more about the rapper’s brother, who got shot.

In this article, we delve into all the details of the alleged shooting incident.

What Happened to Jackboy Brother?

Rapper Jackboy from Haiti is going through a serious dilemma after his brother was allegedly shot several times outside of his Florida house.

Not long after, he was flown to a nearby hospital, where his current status is labeled as “serious.” The unidentified perpetrator left the area in their car.

Prior reports stated that the rapper had been shot, but the performer quickly denied the rumors to Urban Islandz, saying instead that it was his sibling.

“Preliminary investigation revealed that the suspect approached a young adult male who was in his driveway and shot him multiple times. This appears to be a targeted attack, and there is no immediate threat to the surrounding community,” local authorities are reported to have posted on social media, adding, “Please avoid the area as there is a heavy police presence.” It’s still unknown what set off the shooting.”

Shooting of Jackboy Brother Illustrates How Rapper Are Targeted

The shooting of Jackboy’s brother serves as a reminder of the State’s high rate of targeting of rappers and the risks of gun violence that come with their notoriety. As previously said, the gunman intended to hit the rapper, but he shot his brother instead.

Jackboy’s brother was shot on Wednesday morning, directly outside his Florida home, according to reports from Urban Islandz.

In a drive-by shooting, gunmen shot the rapper’s brother many times before fleeing the scene of the crime. According to the authorities, the incident was a targeted crime that may have been well planned out beforehand.

The rapper’s brother was evacuated to a neighboring hospital when the authorities were notified, and this was essential to his survival.

According to reports, his sibling is still fighting for his life in terrible condition. Fans and supporters are hopeful that his brother will make a full recovery.

This year has been difficult for Jackboy. The former Kodak Black affiliate was recently accused of domestic violence by rapper Lexxstasy, which landed him in hot water. Kodak Black, who was recently jailed on possession charges, is currently serving time in Broward County.

“He would beat the sh*t out of me. Spit on me and forced me to sit butt-a* naked in the shower in a sixty-degree house while using a fan. After he beat my a** until it was bloodied, bruised, and my eyes were almost completely closed, he would have me suck d*ck or f*ck him,” Lexxstasy is reported to have said through an Instagram Live.

Serious accusations have been made against Jackboy, and he was publicly fired by Lexxtasy after being taken into custody in September on suspicion of breaking and entering and cyberstalking.

The alleged shooting of Jckboy’s brother is another difficult situation he has to deal with.
