Thuzar Wint Lwin: Wikipedia, Age, and Height

Thuzar Wint Lwin can be found on the official page of Wikipedia. She is a Burmese model and beauty pageant contestant who was crowned Miss Universe Myanmar 2020 and represented Myanmar at the Miss Universe 2020 pageant in Hollywood, Florida, United States.

In the 69th Miss Universe festival, she received the Best National Costume Award. Similarly, she is the first Burmese contestant in Miss Universe history to rank as a Top Finalist.

She is now pursuing a major in English at East Yangon University.

Thuzar is also a pro-democracy activist who used the pageant to encourage the international community to speak out against the military junta, whose armed forces have killed hundreds of dissidents since seizing power in the 2021 Myanmar coup d’état.
