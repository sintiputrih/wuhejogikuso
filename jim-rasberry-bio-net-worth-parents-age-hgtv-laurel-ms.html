Jim Rasberry Married Life

Jim Rasberry is married to his wife Mallorie Rasberry, who is a “Home Town” star. Laurel native, Jim, and his wife Mallorie shared their wedding vows on 15 August 2009. On their 10th anniversary in 2019, Mallorie wrote a lengthy post on her Instagram.

She wrote, “Happy 10th anniversary! @jobforjim I’m in awe of the life we’ve packed in during the past 10 years. At dinner the other night, we had the best time reminiscing over all the things that have transpired since our wedding day and I began writing them down on the paper tablecloth…trips we’ve taken, places we’ve lived, vehicles that have come and gone, family and business milestones, the people we’ve lost along the way…and, our favorite: becoming parents and the excitement for baby #2 (due 2020!). :”

Mallorie concluded the post, “The waiter probably thought I was crazy as I tore the section of the paper tablecloth and put it in my purse at the end of the night… but I’ll cherish that list forever. I love you @jobforjim ♥️”.

They celebrated their 12th wedding anniversary in August 2021. The family currently resides in Laurel, Mississippi.

Mallorie doesn’t appear on every episode. She appeared on one episode of Home Town 2016 and 8 episodes in (2017-2019) where she was credited in special thanks.

As for her job, Mallorie is the owner, VP Purchasing of Laurel Mercantile & and also works as a hot at AirBnB. She has been serving as Controller at Rasberry Producer Group since August 2011. Moreover, she also worked as Treasurer Board of Directors at Laurel Main Street since January 2015.

Mallorie holds a master’s as well as a Bachelors’s of Accountancy from the University of Mississippi.

Additionally, Mallorie is a very “Home Town” host Erin’s best friend and assists her with DIY.

Furthermore, Mallorie is also a keen chef. Her jambalaya recipe can be found in the cookbook sold on the Laurel Mercantile website. In addition, The Family Recipes & Stories (Vol 1) also features her famous dish. Fans have been praising her dish on the internet and wish to have the recipe.

Jim and Mallorie are still going strong together. They shared two kids Lucy and Lottie. Lucy the eldest was born in August 2013 whereas they welcomed Charlotte James “Lottie” Rasberry on 23 March 2020. Lucy is currently studying in third grade.

What Does Jim Rasberry Do For A Living?

Jim Rasberry is currently serving as the president of Rasberry Financial Services. He wrote on his LinkedIn bio, “I work with individuals and businesses to build a sound financial strategy using 401(K)’s, IRA’s, stocks, bonds, mutual funds, REIT’s, annuities and life insurance.”

His company mainly focuses on Investments, Insurance, and Retirement. He shared on his YouTube video, “work with a financial advisor you know, like, and trust”. Also, Jim as a president tries to interact with his clients in a non-business setting. He also made a few videos on his YouTube channel about investing, Using Science to Build Portfolio Risk, Economic Updates, and a few other videos.

The business website of Raspberry Financial Services shared, “​Jim Rasberry believes you shouldn’t need to know fancy financial jargon to manage your money. Knowing the difference between index funds and mutual funds — that’s his job, not yours. He wants his clients to know they’re in good hands when they come to his office. He’ll help them through the process of managing money step by step, and when they leave, they’ll know he put them first because he’s an independent wealth manager and a fiduciary.”

The site reads further, “In addition to holding his Series 6, 7, 63, 66 securities registrations and various state insurance licenses, Jim Rasberry was named a “Leader in Finance” by the Mississippi Business Journal. He’s proud to be located in South Mississippi, and while his firm may be closer to Main Street than Wall Street, his advisors offer the tools to help provide you with a financial strategy that is understandable, transparent, performance-focused, and built to weather financial storms that might arise.”

Some of the other products and services which the company offers are 401(k) Rollovers, 403(b) Planning, 529 College Savings Plans, Business Succession Planning, Estate Planning, Financial Planning, Investment Management, IRAs, IRA Rollovers, Legacy Planning, Medicaid Planning, Retirement Income Planning, and Small Business Owner Leveraged Planning among other services.
