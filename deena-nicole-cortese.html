Deena Nicole Cortese News - Us Weekly

Name:Deena Nicole Cortese

Hometown:New Egypt, NJ

Birthday:January 12, 1987

About

Deena Nicole Cortese appeared on MTV’s Jersey Shore from 2010 to 2012, returning for the spinoff Jersey Shore: Family Vacation. She also appeared on Couples Therapy in 2014, Celebrity Fear Factor in 2018 and Celebrity Family Feud in 2020.

Cortese married Christopher Buckner in 2017, and their son Christopher John “CJ” was born two years later. The couple welcomed their second son, Cameron Theo, in 2021.
