Harlan Crow Health Update- Does He Have Cancer?

Harlan Crow health has been a topic of concern all over the internet. Let’s find out what’s going on regarding Crow’s health.

Real estate developer Harlan Rogers Crow was born in the United States in 1949.

He was raised in Dallas, Texas, and currently serves as chairman of Crow Holdings, which his father, Trammell Crow, created. He contributes significantly to conservative and Republican causes.

The gifts that Crow allegedly gave to Supreme Court Justice Clarence Thomas without disclosing them in violation of the statute on ethics were made public in 2023.

The uproar was exacerbated by concurrently published articles on Crow’s collection of Nazi artifacts.

Harlan Crow Health Update- Does He Have Cancer?

No, Harlan Crow doesn’t have cancer and is the finest as of 2023 and has been donating $500,000 to the conservative political advocacy group Liberty Central, founded by Ginni Thomas.

Crow has previously donated to conservative groups. He is a director at the American Enterprise Institute, a right-wing think organization that advocates for free markets and a strong role for the United States in global policy.

Further, Cow is a member of the George W. Bush Foundation, the Supreme Court Historical Society, and the Hoover Institution’s board of directors.

Transparency USA states Crow Holdings has donated more than $3 million to Republican causes and elections, including Texas Governor Greg Abbott and the conservative Coalition Por For Texas PAC.

However, the amount of Crow’s personal political contributions is unknown. He has previously stated that he does not report contributions for which he is not compelled to do so.

Harlan Crow Career And Political Activities

From 1974 to 1978, Harlan Crow worked as a leasing agent for Trammell Crow Houston Industrial, and from 1978 to 1986, he supervised Trammell Crow Company’s Dallas Office Building development operations.

From 1986 to 1988, he was President of the Wyndham Hotel Company. In 1988, he took over as CEO of Crow Holdings. He is the Company’s current chairman and was previously its CEO.

Crow is a member of the exclusive Bohemian Club for men, and in 1997 he welcomed Clarence Thomas to the group’s yearly Bohemian Grove summer get-together as a guest. Additionally, Wick Allison, a publisher, counts him as a friend and a former business associate.

Harlan Crow Wife Kathy Crow- Who Is Kathy?

Kathy Crow, Harlan Crow’s wife, is a philanthropist. Kathy is on the boards of several educational institutions.

Kathy is a Southern Methodist University Cox School of Business graduate. Similarly, she graduated from Princeton University in 1989.

According to her LinkedIn profile, Crow serves on the boards of trustees and directors of various institutions. She now serves on the boards of St. Mark’s School of Texas, The Hockaday School, and Southern Methodist University.

She also serves on the advisory boards of UT Southwestern Medical Center, the Cox School of Business, the UNC Kenan-Flagler Business School, and Teach For America.

Like her husband, Kathy frequently participates in charitable and philanthropic activities. The pair contributed $5 million in 2014 to construct the Kathy Crow Commons, a shared housing development at SMU.

Kathy is 74 years old and was born in Texas.

On June 25, 1994, Harlan and Kathy exchanged vows to become husband and wife. Their weddings took place in Texas’ Dallas County.

Harlan was about 49 years old when they got married, and Kathy was only 27.
