Juelz Santana Confirms &quot;Born To Lose, Built To Win,&quot; Addresses Vado Signing With DJ Khaled

Juelz Santana will likely have a busy 2013. He has two mixtapes on the way, “All We Got Is Us” and “God Will’n 2.” He also has a new album on the way,Born to Lose, Built to Win. 

“That’s what every thing’s pretty much gearing up to. Just pay attention. I told you,” he explained in an interview with Power 99. “I’m back on they ass…You’ll start hearing music soon.” 

During the interview, Santana also commented on Vado’s recent signing to DJ Khaled’s We the Best imprint.

“Shout out to Vado,” he said. “Shout out to him. Me and Vado always been cool. That was the move he chose to make. I’m cool with people. I’m worried about me at the end of the day. Shout out to him.” 

More from the interview can be heard below. 

Santa is also preparing for music and a possible tour with Dipset. According to Santana, the crew is “on the same page.” “All We Got Is Us” is a “joint mixtape with me and my homies,” according to Juelz. His solo mixtape, “God Will’n 2” is also on the way. it is the follow-up to January’s “God Will’n.”Born to Lose, Built to Win is tentatively scheduled for a late 2013 release.
