A Quick Biography of the Actress

The Canadian actress was born Ella Jonas Farlinger to her parents, Leonard Farlinger and Jennifer Jonas on the 26th day of March 2001 in Little Italy, Toronto Canada. She was raised alongside her brother whose identity is not known to the media outlet.

After her high school education, from King Edward Junior and senior public school, she was enrolled at Etobicoke School of Art where she honed her acting skills.

Ella Jonas Farlinger Started Acting at The Age of Nine

Ella’s passion for acting as stated can be traced to her roots. Her parents have been in the Canadian movie industry for over a decade.

The 22-year-old first made an appearance in 2010 when she was 9 years old. She starred as Jane Morgan in the fantasy television series My Babysitter is a Vampire. Following this, she made an appearance in other movies including I Am Yours (2013), What We Have, Emily (2014), and Saving Hope (2015). 

Ella’s breakthrough came in 2016 when she was given the role of Prissy Andrews in the Anne with the E movie. The actress explored the character and displayed her versatility which earned her more roles in other vampire flicks. While she is now associated with vampire movies, Ella revealed that she can’t handle too much gore in real life. Aside from this, Farlinger’s other notable roles in films and TV shows include;

  • Kristal Clear (2018)
  • Tokens (2019)
  • Falling (2020)
  • A Dog Cried Wolf (2020)
  • Kicking Blood (2021)
