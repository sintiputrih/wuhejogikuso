What Happened To Chad Kinis &amp; Boyfriend Nikko Chua Did They Breakup?

On April Fool’s Day, Chad Kinis announced the end of his relationship with Nikko Alano Chua after only 10 months together.

Did Chad Kinis & Boyfriend Nikko Chua Breakup? 

Chad Kinis broke up with his boyfriend, Nikko Alano Chua, after being together for barely ten months. 

April 1 is regarded as the day for pranks; however, Chad Kinis was not joking when he broke up with his boyfriend Nikko on April Fool’s Day. Chad had hinted at their breakup eleven days prior.

In his Road to Shrimp vlog on May 21, 2022, Kinis said that he makes his body irresistible so that he is not traded for men who have beautiful bodies.

Additionally, he said that all people have to do is make their bodies appealing so their partners can see what they wasted. His statement appears to imply that Nikko was dating or involved romantically with someone else.

Kinis may have waited until April Fool’s Day because he felt duped in the relationship and had higher expectations. Nikko revealed their relationship officially on Facebook on June 10, 2021, expressing that he will always be there for him no matter what.

Chad Kinis Age – How Old Is He?

Chad Kinis is 36 years old as he was born on April 22, 1986, in Lipa City, Batangas, Philippines.

The 5’9” actor began his career as a stand-up comedian in Metro Manila’s clubs. He was almost unknown before he was invited to appear as a guest on the iconic ABS-CBN noontime show “It’s Showtime” in 2018.
