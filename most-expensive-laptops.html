If you have the money to go for an expensive laptop, you should definitely take a look at our list of 15 Most Expensive Laptops in the World in 2021.

15. Razer Blade Stealth

Price: $2,000

Graphic processor and gaming prowess are some of the unique features of a Razer gaming laptop.

The Razer Blade Stealth has an Intel i7 11th gen Core processor, which caters for its extreme gaming performance. 

It also has a 28W powerful GeForce GTX 1650 Ti, which is the GPU and provides for the gaming, designing needs of the user.

The 120Hz rate in the display is perfect for gaming with zero loss, along with an OLED touch screen.

The performance of the 11th gen processors allows for the base clock speed to reach around 2.8Ghz and 4.7 GHz, which is more than enough for all users.

The laptop’s sleek design and overall features are perfect for any gaming and productivity setup for the users.

The utility and other uses provided by Razer Blade Stealth makes it worth the $2,000.

14. Acer Ferrari 1100

Price: $3,000

The automobile manufacturer “Ferrari” is the inspiration for the name Acer Ferrari 1100. The performance and speed of the Acer Ferrari 1100 are unmatched by any other laptop.

Despite its small size (12.1-inch display), it packs quite a punch in speed and performance. The prominent logo of Ferrari is visible on the front of the laptop.

It has ATI Radeon graphics of X1270 and 4GB RAM for processing.

Other specifications include the 2.3 GHz AMD Turion 64 and the 250 GB storage, making it quite accessible.

Another important feature is it is lightweight (4.4 pounds).

Ferrari’s fabulous features and unique logo make Acer Ferarri 1100 cost up to $3,000.

13. Dell M6400 Laptop

Price: $3,000

The Dell M6400 is the ideal “Power Workstation.”

The flagship M6400 model can deliver for the needs of the most demanding users.

It is equipped with the Intel Quad-core processing power, which combines with the super-fast 1066 MHz FSB, in part with the Fire Pro and NVIDIA graphics.

The sleek design allows the M6400 to be the ideal mobile workstation, with a 17-inch display and 100% Adobe color gamut.

The easy scalability options also make it handy.

The memory is scalable up to 16 GB. The easy-to-use trackpad, media reader, and microphone availability make it convenient for daily work purposes. Dell M6400 caters to the needs of most corporate users.

The proper utility and other performance features add to the price tag of $3,000.

12. Lenovo ThinkPad W700DS

Price: $4,500

The latest processors and graphics options are available in the Lenovo ThinkPad W700DS.

The large display of (17 inches) is the most defining feature of the W700DS version.

The graphics and processor are of cutting edge technology which makes the W700DS the ‘Ultimate Mobile Workstation.’

The Intel Core 2 Extreme Q9300 2.53 GHz CPU and 4GB RAM are a few of the special features of W700DS.

In terms of performance, the larger “Ultranav” stylus pad makes work, basic tasks much easier.

Despite its performance, the bulky size does bring in portability issues.

The Lenovo ThinkPad W700DS has a price tag of $4,500, making it the 12th most expensive laptop globally.

11. Alienware Area 51 M15X

Price: $5,000

Most people who know about laptops have heard about the “Alienware” brand.

The Alienware Area 51 M15X needs no introduction when it comes to its graphic and processing prowess.

The M15X is the perfect portable gaming notebook.

It comes equipped with a 2.8Ghz Extreme Core 2 Duo processor and works with a 4GB DDR2 Intel chipset.

The graphics onboard is the NVIDIA 8800 M GTX which caters to most avid users’ gaming and designing needs.

In terms of display, the M15X has a true HD resolution of 1920*1200 and a matte display to reduce lighting glare.

Another unique feature is the lighting scheme (Alien FX), which allows up to 7 different light colors in the laptop’s cover, keyboard, and touchpad.

Packed with unique features and extreme performance levels, the Alienware Area 51 M15X costs around $5,000, making it one of the most expensive laptops in the world.

10. Alienware 18

Price: $5,400

Another beast in terms of graphics and performance is the Alienware 18.

It comes equipped with an Intel Core i7-4800MQ processor, and the GPU is NVIDIA GeForce GTX 780M, which is very useful for gamers and graphic designers.

The massive display of 18.4 inches comes with a full HD, making it ample for movies and gaming purposes.

The 32GB RAM DDR3, 1600 Mhz caters to the most experienced users’ processing needs.

The storage of 256 GB SSD and 750 GB HDD makes it handy for users to store their important stuff.

Few of the avid users look down upon the heavyweight (5.8Kg) of the laptop.

A beast in both performance and size, the Alienware 18 costs up to $5,400.

9. MacBook Pro Marble Edition

Price: $7,500

In terms of performance and utility, MacBooks are well known globally.

But, MacBook Pro Marble Edition displays class and status through its unique design features.

It is designed by ColorWare, which redesigned the normal MacBook Pro and handcrafted it into a more earthly ‘marble’ look.

The 24 Karat Apple gold logo completes the unique Marble design of the laptop.

ColorWare ensures maximum quality through their process of disassembly, cleaning, masking, painting, and inspection to ensure proper packaging.

Its rarity is also one of the reasons for the expensive price tag.

To date, only a limited 10 pieces of the MacBook Marble edition has been designed and released.

The MacBook Pro Marble Edition costs up to $7,500, making it the 9th most expensive laptop globally.

8. Voodoo Envy H171

Price: $8,500

The Voodoo Envy H171 is the “New Standards For Notebook,” according to its developers.

It features Core 2 Extreme CPU, Dual NVIDIA GeForce Graphics, and 4GB RAM.

Its unique features include hard drive storage to almost 600 GB.

Since its release in 2017, it has created a new topic of conversation among tech enthusiasts.

During its release, it was the most powerful gaming laptop.

The Voodoo Envy H171 can cater to the needs of gaming, data science, and designing of most users.

The 250 units limited edition production is another reason for its expensive pricing.

The Voodoo Envy H171 has a price tag of $8,500, making it the 8th most expensive laptop globally.

7. Rock Xtreme SL8

Price: $18,000

Rock Xtreme SL8 is another laptop that appeals to gaming enthusiasts.

It is one of the fastest gaming laptops of all time. 

The massive 17-inch screen with a display of 1920*1200 pixels provides proper resolution for gaming.

It comes with an Intel Core 2 Quad processor and NVIDIA GeForce 9800M GTX SLI graphics card.

The Xtreme SL8 is a very good replacement for a powerful gaming desktop setup for any gamer.

The functionality and feature-packed Rock Xtreme SL8 cost around $18,000, making it the 7th most expensive laptop globally.

6. Ego For Bentley

Price: $19,000

Ego For Bentley was launched in partnership with Bentley and Ego Lifestyle as limited edition luxury laptops.

It comes equipped with a 64-bit processor and a 180GB hard drive.

Only 250 limited pieces are available at high-end stores globally.

The Ego For Bentley is quite known for its awesome performance, handcrafted materials, and the luxury it defines.

The laptop indeed reflects the partnership between the two companies in terms of function, form, and status.

Similarly, the laptop also has an ergonomic handle, making it easier to carry and resembling a leather bag.

The iconic Bentley logo, along with the leather top, makes it even more attractive and unique.

The Ego For Bentley has a price tag of $19,000, making it a status symbol in terms of laptops worldwide.

Price: $20,000

Tulip E-Go Diamond Notebook is another laptop that screams “Money!”.

The design resembles a purse which is quite similar to “Ego for Bentley.”

Changeable skins, integrated webcam, Auto DJ features are unique to Tulip E-Go.

The manufacturer describes Tulip E-Go as an “Electronic Jewelry with the latest technology.” 

It comes equipped with an AMD Turion 64-Bit CPU and display of 12 inches with has anti-glare display technology.

The white gold plates and diamonds on the logo are another factor increasing the price tag of the product.

A unique ruby is used to design the tulip logo on top of the laptop.

The fashionable laptop Tulip E-Go Diamond Notebook cost around $20,000, making it the 5th most expensive laptop globally.

4. MacBook Pro 24K Gold

Price: $30,000

Owning MacBook Pro is impressive, but owning a MacBook Pro covered in gold is a something else.

The 24K gold edition was only designed 99 units.

The Apple logo is designed on the front with diamonds and 24K gold.

Adaptations in rose gold and other valuable stones are also available, which may be used on the logo.

The display has bright colors, great contrast, broad viewing angles and provides an astonishing 5.1 million pixels.

The MacBook Pro 24K Gold costs around $30,000, making it the 4th most expensive laptop of all time.

3. MacBook Air Supreme

Price: $500,000

The MacBook Air Supreme consists of 53 individual sparkling gems engraved in the Apple logo.

It weighs almost 2.6Kg which is twice that of its normal base model.

The Intel Core 2 Duo processor is equipped with 2.13 GHz 2GB and 128 GB of SSD memory.

The MacBook Air Supreme takes up to 3 months to construct.

If you want a laptop that shows your love for diamonds, MacBook Air Supreme is the one to go for.

The beauty Air Supreme model costs up to $500,000, making it the 3rd most expensive laptop in the world.

2. Luvaglio

Price: $1 Million

Because of the heavy price tag, Luvagilo is sometimes called a “Million Dollar Laptop.”

It is provided with a large 17 inch LCD and is loaded with features such as Blu-Ray, USB, and 128 GB SSD.

Despite its price tag, the Luvagilo has average performance scores.

It is designed more towards exclusivity and value.

There is a diamond start button on the laptop, and yet you are using the same old metal start button?

Jokes apart, the Luvaglio has a whopping price tag of $1 million, making it the 2nd most expensive laptop globally.

1. MJ’s Swarovski and Diamond Studded Notebook

Price: $3.5 Million

MJ Swarovski and Diamond Studded Notebook is the definition of “as expensive as it gets.”

It makes use of rhinestone and crystal beads which are of top quality.

Swarvoski makes use of the best rhinestone with the best color, clarity, and sparkle for their customers.

The specifications are top-notch, but the customizations are what racks up the price.

The raw materials used, the meticulous design and implementation, and the brand name make the laptop expensive.
