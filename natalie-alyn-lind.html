Natalie Alyn Lind is a Canadian actress who is known for playing Lauren Strucker in the Fox superhero series The Gifted, Dana Caldwell in the ABC period sitcom series The Goldbergs, Ashley Rose Pruitt in the psychological thriller anthology television series Tell Me A Story, Mavis in the Netflix post-apocalyptic comedy-drama series Daybreak, Danielle Sullivan in the ABC crime drama thriller series Big Sky, and Silver St. Cloud in the Fox superhero crime drama series Gotham. In addition to that, Natalie Alyn Lind has also been a part of various other on-screen projects including shows and movies like One Tree Hill, Army Wives, Flashpoint, Criminal Minds, iCarly, November Christmas, Blood Done Sign My Name, Wizards of Waverly Place, Kaboom, Dear Dumb Diary, Murder in the First, Mockingbird, Chicago Fire, iZombie, and Playdate.
