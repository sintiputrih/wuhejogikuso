Josh Hartnett Partner, Spouse, Wife, Girlfriends &amp; Net Worth

Who is Josh Hartnett?

For his roles in Pearl Harbor and Black Hawk Down, Actor Josh Hartnett has successfully ruled the heart of the huge mass. He also appeared in 40 Days and 40 Nights, The Virgin Suicides, and O. He starred in Penny Dreadful's horror series from 2014 to 2016. 

Josh Hartnett was born in Saint Paul, Minnesota, the USA on July 21, 1978. He is the son of Daniel Hartnett and Wendy Anne. Although he was born to Wendy Anne, he grew up with his dad and stepmother. Hartnett has three siblings named Jake Hartnett and Joe Hartnett and Jessica Hartnett. 

Josh holds an American nationality and belongs to mixed ethnicity (Swedish, Irish, German, Norwegian). Moving towards his education, he went to Our Lord Catholic School of Nativity. Later, Josh graduated from the Conservatory of Theater Arts & Film at SUNY Purchase. 

In the 1998 short film Debutante, Josh started his acting career as Bill. He also appeared in two more movies in the same year, Halloween H20: 20 Years Later and The Faculty. Soon after that, in films like Here on Earth, The Same, Member, Blow Dry, and Town & Country, he gained numerous roles. He has also collaborated with actors like Ben Affleck, Tom Hardy, Scarlett Johansson and many more.

As of now, Josh has won several awards and nomination such as the ShoWest Award, the Milano International Film Festival Awards, and the Acting Award for Ischia International Arts Academy.
