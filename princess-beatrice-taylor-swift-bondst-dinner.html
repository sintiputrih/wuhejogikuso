Princess Beatrice, Taylor Swift Both Dined at BondSt Sushi in NYC Saturday Night

Turns out Taylor Swift and Princess Beatrice have similar taste in New York City restaurants: The pop superstar and the British royal were both spotted at NoHo sushi restaurant BondSt on Saturday evening.

Gotham//Getty Images

Swift holds hands with Mahomes, left, and links arms with Gomez, right. Behind her are Hadid and Turner.

Swift was joined by many of her friends that evening, including Selena Gomez, Sophie Turner, Gigi Hadid, Cara Delevingne, and new pal Brittany Mahomes (wife of Kansas City Chiefs quarterback Patrick Mahomes), whereas Princess Beatrice was snapped entering the restaurant solo. Her husband, Edo Mapelli Mozzi, was not pictured with her, but two days ago, she attended an event in the city with her mom, Sarah Ferguson, Duchess of York. For that outing, Beatrice was in royal-loved brand the Vampire’s Wife.

Zimmermann Asymmetric Ruffled Silk-Satin Midi Wrap Dress

At BondSt, Princess Beatrice wore a Burberry trench coat, a satin Zimmermann wrap dress, and velvet Jimmy Choo heels. Swift, meanwhile, kept up her autumnal style streak, wearing a long-sleeved black shirt, a Miu Miu skirt, and Stella McCartney boots.

Though we do not know if the princess is a Swiftie, another member of the royal family, Meghan Markle, reportedly saw the singer on her Eras Tour in Los Angeles this past summer. (Swift opens the next, international leg of the tour this weekend in Argentina; she’ll perform in the U.K. throughout June 2024.)

It’s unclear if Beatrice and Swift interacted inside the restaurant, or if they were even seated near each other. Still, what are the odds they would be in the same room at the same time? Perhaps the princess is—as Swift sings on Midnights—a “mastermind.”
