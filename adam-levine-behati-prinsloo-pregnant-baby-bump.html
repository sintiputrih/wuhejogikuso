Adam Levine Just Shared a Photo of Behati Prinsloo's Baby Bump

In an extra-special Valentine's Day snap, Adam Levine proved just how excited he is about having a second baby with his wife, Behati Prinsloo.

Levine and Prinsloo welcomed their first child in September 2016, a daughter named Dusty Rose Levine.

In November 2017, Levine discussed his wife's second pregnancy on The Ellen DeGeneres Show, revealing, "It is a girl. We are having another girl." Clearly, the Maroon 5 singer is extremely excited about expanding his family further.

He took the opportunity on Valentine's Day to share the sweetest photo of his wife and her bare baby bump, with the caption, "My maniac," followed by a lot of heart emojis:

Prinsloo is lying in bed atop a leopard print blanket, with a gorgeous box of chocolates alongside her. A vase of pink roses sit on the bedside table, presumably from Levine. With their second on the way, the couple seems very loved up.

Prinsloo and Levine started dating in May 2012, and got married two years later in July 2014 in Mexico.

In February 2017, Levine gushed about Dusty and Prinsloo at his Hollywood Walk of Fame ceremony:
