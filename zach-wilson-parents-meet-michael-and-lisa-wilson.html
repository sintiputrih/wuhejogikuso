Zach Wilson Parents: Meet Michael and Lisa Wilson

Zachary Wilson is an American football quarterback for the National Football League’s New York Jets (NFL).

Wilson was born on August 3, 1999, in Draper, Utah, to Michael and Lisa Wilson. he has three brothers and two sisters.

On his father’s side, he is Hawaiian, and his middle name, Kapono, means “righteous” in Hawaiian.

Lisa and Mike raised their six children according to Mormon principles; the siblings have a strong bond based on their religious beliefs. In March 2022, Lisa and Mike celebrated their 25th wedding anniversary.

Mike Wilson is a businessman who owns and operates several gas stations and other businesses in the Salt Lake City area.

Lisa describes herself as a “fitness fanatic,” and she teaches dance at various Utah fitness centres such as Studio IVL and Treehouse Fitness.

Lisa is also a chef who specializes in healthy recipes and hosts classes at her home in Utah, according to her Instagram.

Lisa has her own company, Elite 4 Apparel, which sells merchandise for her ‘Bad Moms Club,’ such as slogan t-shirts, and appears to work with several companies that offer discount codes for products.
