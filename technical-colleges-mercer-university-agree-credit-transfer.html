Technical colleges, Mercer University agree on credit transfer

Students who graduate from most campuses within the Technical College System of Georgia can transfer their credits toward a bachelor's degree in nine programs offered by Mercer University, under an agreement leaders signed Thursday. The agreement applies to students who attend one of the 19 technical colleges accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges. The system has 25 colleges. Information about the Mercer programs can be found at www.mercer.edu.
