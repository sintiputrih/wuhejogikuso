Who Is Halseys Boyfriend Alev Aydin, And What Does He Do For A Living?

Halsey has been crushing the game when it comes to her music, however the singer is now shifting her focus over to motherhood after revealing she's expecting her first child back in February.

In celebration of her pregnancy, Halsey revealed shots from her maternity shoot, showing off her many tattoos, newest hairdo, and of course, growing baby bump! Although fans know Alev is the baby daddy, they certainly want to know more! So, who is Alev Aydin? Let's find out!

Who Is Alev Aydin?

Halsey has kept quite a low profile since her debut back in 2015 when she released her first studio album, Badlands. The singer has since come out with a number of hit songs that have allowed her to become one of the biggest names in pop.

While it's clear her music career has taken off and then some, it appears as if her personal life is also experiencing some major changes. In February 2021, Halsey revealed that she was officially expecting her first child.

The baby is set to be born this upcoming August to parents Halsey and her boyfriend, Alev Aydin. The couple made their first-ever public appearance as a couple back in October 2020 in Los Angeles, sparking rumors that things were getting very serious between the two.

Well, flash forward a few months and not only are they dating, but they are starting a family together! While we know the extent of their relationship, fans still have a lot of questions regarding who exactly Alev Aydin is.

For starters, Alev is quite familiar with the entertainment business as he is a screenwriter! Aydin also reigns from Turkey, which he has referred to as his "homeland" a number of times on social media.

His true work remains in the film and television sphere and has come to be quite a success. In 2017, Alev released an eight-episode television series he created called Small Shots, which is available to stream on Netflix!

In addition to the success of Small Shots, Aydin has also created Turbo and Joey, which he released back in 2013, and a short film titled HipMen: Los Angeles. 

As if his creative side weren't enough, Alev is also a major sports fan, much like Halsey! The duo have bonded quite a lot over their love for basketball, which they have both been spotted at courtside.

While the two remain private about their relationship, it's become clear that they are a perfect couple! As they head on their pregnancy journey together, fans can only expect an engagement of sorts sooner than later, and we don't blame 'em!
