Should well written be hyphenated? If the term “well written” precedes the noun, it is a compound adjective and requires a comma: “It is a well-written essay.” However, when it comes after the noun, no hyphen: “The essay was well written.” Is well understood hyphenated? For example, a “well understood concept” is not hyphenated (well is an adverb modifying the adjective understood). Do you hyphenate well received?
