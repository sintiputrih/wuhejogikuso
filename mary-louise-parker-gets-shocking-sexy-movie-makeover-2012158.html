Mary-Louise Parker Goes Blonde, Wears Plunging Swimsuit in Sexy Makeover

Kids, cover your eyes!

Mary-Louise Parker debuted a new bombshell look Tuesday in Los Angeles — but the Weeds star won’t be keeping it up for long. It’s just for her new flick, Parental Guidance Suggested.

Filming scenes for the flick — in which she plays a matador named Saint Lola — Parker, 48, donned a sexy plunging gold swimsuit, wrap skirt and chic platinum blonde bob wig. She accessorized her look with an embellished red and gold matador jacket and gold shin guards.

Her movie makeover is a far cry from Parker’s normal wavy locks, which she sports to play stoner mom Nancy Botwin on Showtime’s Weeds, which wraps its eighth and final season this year.
