All map locations &amp; what they do

There are plenty of quests to complete in Hello Kitty Island Adventure, and getting the Blue Power Crystals is another quest that you can complete. This is part of the Comic Relief quest. Here is how you can complete the Comic Relief quest for Hangyodon in the game.

Where to find all Blue Power Crystals in Hello Kitty Island Adventure

Hangyodon has opened a Comedy Club, but the twist is that it’s underwater. He is the only fish in the group, so at the moment, no one else can make it to the Comedy Club. To help him with his Comedy Club, you need to find three Blue Power Crystals so that he can rebuild the mermaidifier.

In order to complete this quest, you’ll also have to have unlocked the Snorkel (as this is how you find Hangyodon) and reach friendship level 6 with Hangyodon. 

The first Blue Power Crystal will be found by the dock on the south shore of Gemstone Mountain. There are two ways to get this crystal. Get to the dock from the Pirate Ship underwater and go southwest, or walk across the boardwalk south until you find the dock. Then you’ll need to dive into the water and dive to the bottom of the trench. You’ll find the Blue Power Crystal floating on coral.

The second Blue Power Crystal is inside the Pirate Ship. Go into the south side of the ship, where you see the hole in the hull. Swim into the hole and you’ll find a secret room. There will be a puzzle for you to complete, where you need to use your tools at your disposal to get to the top of the mast where you’ll be “rewarded” with the Blue Power Crystal.

The final Blue Power Crystal is in the Kelp Maze. This is northeast of the Comedy Club. Swim out to the stone ridges that form “L’s” in the water. In the middle of this, you’ll be able to find the final blue power crystal.

Head back to Hangyodon and place the blue crystals into the Mermaidifier, which will turn your friends into mermaids and allow them to access the Comedy Club.

That’s everything you need to know about how to find the Blue Power Crystals in Hello Kitty Island Adventure.
