Who is Professional Sports Authenticators (PSA)?

published on August 28, 2024
Professional Sports Authenticators (PSA) is a company that provides authentication and grading services for sports collectibles. PSA was founded in 1991 by a group of collectors who saw a need for a reliable, third-party evaluation of sports memorabilia.PSA uses a team of experts to carefully examine sports items, such as trading cards and autographed jerseys, and assigns them a grade based on their condition.
