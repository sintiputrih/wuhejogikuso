Mary J. Blige Net Worth, Age, Boyfriend, Family, Biography &amp; More

Mary Jane Blige, popularly known as Mary J. Blige is an American who is a popular singer, songwriter, and actress by profession. Her music genres are R&B and hip-hop soul. She is also very famous for “Queen of hip hop soul”.

She is the winner of 4 American Music Awards, Ten Billboard Music Awards, and Nine Grammy Awards. Apart from it, she has also got a nomination for Two Academy and Three Golden Globe awards. She was signed by Uptown records in the year 1991.

Mary J. Blige Wiki/Biography

Born on 11 January 1971, Mary J. Blige’s age is 51 years as of 2022. She was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Yonkers, New York, the United States. She is an American by nationality and has a belief in the Christian religion.

She completed her early schooling at Roosevelt High School.

After that, she enrolled herself at a Local College from where she completed her graduation. From the very beginning of her childhood, she was more interested in Singing, Acting, and various co-curricular activities rather than in studying.
