Irina Shayk Reminds Everyone Shes Still Dating Bradley Cooper With This Sexy Photo

Steamy romance, indeed! Irina Shayk posted a racy hot-tub selfie of herself and boyfriend Bradley Cooper to Instagram on Friday, April 8, after nearly one year of dating.

In the sexy photo, the 30-year-old Russian beauty poses at the forefront of the snapshot with her cleavage front and center in a low-cut, lace-up blue swimsuit. A man who appears to be Cooper, 41, sits behind her shirtless, with water up to both their chests.

Though the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit model cropped both of their faces out of the photo, the tiny curl of the Limitless actor’s hair at the nape of his neck and his scruff are unmistakeable.

The low-key couple made their first red carpet appearance together just last month at the L’Oréal Red Obsession party during Paris Fashion Week on March 8.

The pair kept their arms around each other and even sneaked a few kisses while inside the star-studded fete.

Cooper and Shayk were first linked in April 2015, and several months later displayed some major PDA while enjoying a beach day on Italy’s Amalfi Coast in August.

Though neither has spoken publicly about their relationship, it appears to have gotten pretty serious pretty quickly, with the model meeting the Oscar winner’s mother, Gloria Campano, in September.
