Meet Barracuda Queens Actress Alva Bratt

Alva Bratt (age 24, September 28, 1998) is a stunning Swedish actress who has been making waves in the entertainment industry. She is just 24 years old but has already achieved great success in her career.

Alva first caught everyone’s attention with her breakthrough role as Felicia Kroon in the popular series “Eagles,” released in 2019.

In “Eagles,” She portrayed the character of Felicia Kroon for an impressive 37 episodes until 2022. Her performance in the series garnered widespread attention and opened doors for her career.

Building on her success, Alva’s acting journey reached new heights in 2022 when she landed the fabulous role of Augustina in “Kronprinsen som försvann.” This opportunity further solidified her position as a rising star in the industry.

Continuing her ascent to stardom, Alva recently landed another lead role in the Netflix series “Barracuda Queens,” in which she appeared as Lollo Millkvist, and this role has propelled her fame to even greater heights.

But her rise to fame will not stop there because director Sigge Eklund, recognizing her talent, has already cast her in his upcoming fled “A Part of You” (En del av dig). This exciting opportunity will undoubtedly bring Alva even more recognition and success.

Early Life & Parents

Alva Bratt, born on September 28, 1998, had a creative upbringing with her mother, Cajsa Bratt, and father, Pontus Frankenstein. Her parents are well-known designers, and her father, Pontus, is the proud owner of Frankenstein Studio, a design and advertising agency.

During her formative years, she attended Södra Latin Secondary School in Stockholm and later pursued her studies at Ecole Française French School, where she expanded her knowledge and skills.

Alva’s passion for the performing arts blossomed from a young age. Initially, she aspired to become a director in the film and theater industry.

However, her journey took a delightful turn when she enrolled in a theater program during high school. It was during this time that she discovered her love for acting.

Even before high school, Bratt had dipped her toes into the theater world. She attended Kalle Flygare’s theater school. At age 15, Alva played the role of Juliet’s mother in a production of “Romeo & Juliet.”

Alva Bratt Acting Journey

Alva’s acting journey began with a focus on theater, but she stopped doing theater programs after completing her graduation at Södra Latin. Little did she know that this choice would lead her to an incredible turning point two years later.

In 2019, everything changed for Alva when she landed the “Felicia Kroon” role in the TV series “Eagles.” This debut role catapulted her into the limelight, and she portrayed Felicia Kroon for a remarkable four seasons, capturing the hearts of audiences until 2022.

During her time on “Eagles,” She also showcased her talent in “Biggest of All,” “The zebra room,” “Lasse-Maja’s detective agency,” “Grown-ups,” and “The Crown Prince who disappeared.” These diverse roles allowed her to hone her skills further and gain valuable experience.

However, in 2023, Alva again began to shine brightly as she captivated audiences with her heart-winning performance in the series “Barracuda Queens.
