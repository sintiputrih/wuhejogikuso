Career

Amy began her career through modelling. After she won the Miss teen Liverpool and Miss Teen Great Britain Pageants, she went on to win the Miss Teen World in 2009. After being enrolled as a model through her modelling agency Models 1, she made her debut in acting a Tamil movie titled Madrasapattinam.

It was a period film and despite having no acting training or background, Amy delivered a groundbreaking performance and was oraidrd for her acting abilities and talent.

She even won the Vijaya Award for her performance in her debut movie. After that she was signed by director Gautham Menon to play the leading actress in a movie called Ekk Deewana tha.

She was paired with Prateik Babbar. In 2012 she returned to Tamil cinema with another movie called Thaandavam in which she was cast with Anushka Shetty and Vikram. This film was a success and Amy was praised for her performance in the movie.

After this Amy made her debut in Telugu cinema with a movie called Yevadu in 2014. After that she was cast in another big budget Tamil thriller titled I. After that in 2015 she signed a movie called Singh is Bliing with Akshay Kumar.

In the same year she signed another Tamil movie called Thanga Magan. The movie also starred Samantha Ruth Prabhu and Dhanush. In 2016 she had more Tamil releases namely Gethu and Atlees Theri.
