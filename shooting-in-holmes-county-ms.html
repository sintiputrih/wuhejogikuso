Shooting in Holmes County MS: A close-knit neighborhood in Goodman, Holmes County, Mississippi, is reeling from the shock of a horrific drive-by shooting that occurred on Wednesday. Drive-by Shooting in Holmes County, Mississippi

A tragic incident unfolded in Goodman, Holmes County, Mississippi, when a drive-by shooting took place on Wednesday at noon. This shocking act of violence occurred near the BP petrol station on Highway 51, leaving the close-knit neighborhood in a state of disbelief and concern.

Incident Details

The drive-by shooting resulted in the loss of one life and left three individuals critically injured. The identity of the victim has been confirmed as Clyzavion Landfair, a 17-year-old. Despite the efforts of medical professionals, Landfair succumbed to his injuries at a nearby hospital, devastating his family and friends. To gain a comprehensive understanding of the incident, the Holmes County Coroner will conduct a thorough autopsy at the State Medical Examiner’s office.
