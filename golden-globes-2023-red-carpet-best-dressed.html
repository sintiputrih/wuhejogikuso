29 of the best red carpet looks from the 2023 Golden Globes

The 80th Golden Globes will honor the best in television and film

    The 80th Golden Globes officially kicked off awards season on Tuesday and with it came the welcomed return of the red carpet.

    RELATED: Ryan Murphy's 11 best TV shows of all-time

    Despite going on hiatus last year amid controversy, the awards honoring the best in television and film took up residence for the three-hour ceremony at the Beverly Hilton Hotel in Beverly Hills – and the star-studded guestlist did not disappoint with their jaw-dropping red carpet looks.

    WATCH: Stars hit red carpet for 80th annual Golden Globes

    Celebrities in attendance that brought some serious glamour to the event included Salma Hayek, Lily James, Jenna Ortega, Angela Bassett, Margot Robbie, Selena Gomez, Jennifer Coolidge, and so many more.

    Check out the best-dressed stars on the red carpet below…

    2023 Golden Globes red carpet

    Lily James

    Lily James stood out in this jaw-dropping custom Atelier Versace gown and De Beers jewelry.

    Salma Hayek

    Salma Hayek looked beautiful in her beaded, nude Gucci gown, Gucci heels, and Maison Boucheron jewelry.

    Jenna Ortega

    Jenna Ortega looked world's away from her alter-ego in this dreamy Gucci dress, Jimmy Choo shoes, and Tiffany & Co. jewelry.

    Michelle Williams

    Michelle Williams rocked romantic ruffles in head-to-toe Gucci and Tiffany & Co. jewelry.

    Angela Bassett

    star Angela Bassett stunned in her sparkly silver Pamella Roland dress, Sarah Flint shoes, and Chopard jewelry.

    Julia Garner

    star Julia Garner made a splash in pink Gucci and De Beers jewelry.

    Viola Davis

    Viola Davis didn't let her rain-soaked dress ruin her red carpet entrance in this bold blue custom creation by Jason Wu, Sole Bliss shoes, and Chopard jewelry.

    Jessica Chastain

    Jessica Chastain stole the show in her exquisite Oscar de la Renta dress and Gucci jewelry.

    Margot Robbie

    Margot Robbie was a vision in custom Chanel.

    Anya Taylor-Joy

    star Anya Taylor-Joy stood out in her vibrant Christian Dior ensemble.

    Jennifer Hudson

    Jennifer Hudson made sure all eyes were on her in her gold metallic frock by C.D. Greene, Giuseppe Zanotti heels, and Pomellato jewelry.

    Selena Gomez

    nominee Selena Gomez looked incredible in Valentino Haute Couture.

    Ana de Armas

    star Ana de Armis rocked custom Louis Vuitton from head to toe and looked gorgeous!

    Jennifer Coolidge

    star Jennifer Coolidge looked beautiful in a figure-hugging, black sequin gown.

    Daisy Edgar-Jones

    star Daisy Edgar-Jones wowed in her eye-catching, lingerie-inspired gown with a ruffled, tiered skirt.

    Heidi Klum

    star and supermodel Heidi Klum grabbed attention in this silver, sequin, cut-out mini dress with purple feather embellishment by Kevin Germanier, Tom Ford heels and Lorraine Schwartz jewelry.

    Jamie Lee Curtis

    Jamie Lee Curtis was a vision in a black, strapless Safiyaa jumpsuit and a black, lace cape by Valentino.

    Kaley Cuoco

    Pregnant Kaley Cuoco showcased her growing baby bump in a pretty lavender Vera Wang Haute gown.

    Laverne Cox

    star Laverne Cox looks gorgeous in vintage John Galliano and Fred Leighton and Kwiat jewelry.

    Rhea Seehorn

    star Rhea Seehorn stunned in this floral, sequinned frock by Naeem Khan and jewelry from Anne Sisteron and Mindi Mond.

    Monica Barbaro

    actress Monica Barbaro oozed glamour in red Dolce & Gabbana and Giuseppe Zanotti shoes.

    Milly Alcock

    star Milly Alcock kept it simple but stunning in Givenchy, Gianvito Rossi heels, and Pomellato jewelry.

    Bailey Bass

    and actress Bailey Bass was a vision in classic Dior.

    Hannah Bagshawe

    Eddie Redmayne's wife Hannah Bagshawe went for full-out glam in this low-back Valentino gown with a train, accessorizing with a classic Omega watch.

    Monica Ahanonu

    Illustrator Monica Ahanonu looked breathtaking in her tulle, ruffled gown with extended train.

    Hilary Swank

    Hilary Swank highlighted her baby bump in this beautiful green Prada dress and Tiffany & Co. jewelry.

    Natasha Lyonne

    star Natasha Lyonne looked elegant in Givenchy.

    Niecy Nash

    star Niecy Nash looked regal in her purple, jewel-embellished dress with a ruffled cape by Dolce & Gabbana.

    Claire Danes

    actress Claire Danes opted for this quirky Giambattista Valli Haute Couture outfit.
