Becky Lynch Roasts Her Own Dance Moves at Eminem Fortnite Concert

Becky Lynch’s hard work in WWE has paid off in many ways, as she is a multi-time women’s champion. Lynch has proved how good she is, but that didn’t translate to her dancing skills. In fact, Lynch decided to roast her own dance moves at the Fortnite Eminem concert.

Enimem’s collaboration with Fortnite culminated in a spectacular concert during Fortnite’s Big Bang event, which took place on December 2nd. Naturally, fans had a lot of fun watching the concert and taking part in it in the virtual world.

A video surfaced from the concert, which showed Becky Lynch’s in-game model dancing while Eminem was performing at the concert. Lynch took to Twitter and roasted her own dance moves after watching the clip, in a moment that fans loved to see.

”When I thought I couldn’t get any worse at dancing, Fortnite made it happen.”

Becky Lynch also hilariously owned up to her ‘Celebrity Jeopardy’ blunder. Regardless, fans always appreciate Big Time Becks’ sense of humor. It remains to be seen what WWE has planned for Lynch next.
