16 Years of Welcome: Akshay Kumar introduces Sanjay Dutt in Welcome to the Jungle

Welcome, the 2007 film starring Akshay Kumar, Katrina Kaif, Nana Patekar, Anil Kapoor and others is getting a third instalment with Welcome to the Jungle. As the film completes 16 years since its release, Akshay Kumar shared a sneak peek into the upcoming film.

Akshay Kumar offered a glimpse of Welcome to the Jungle featuring himself alongside Sanjay Dutt. In the video, Akshay can be seen on a horse while Sanjay is riding a bike. Check it out here:

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Akshay Kumar (@akshaykumar)

Sharing the clip on his social media handle, Akshay Kumar wrote, “What a lovely coincidence that we celebrate 16 years of #Welcome today while I am shooting for the franchise’s third part #WelcomeToTheJungle. And it’s wonderful to welcome Sanju Baba in this one. What do you think? @duttsanjay #Welcome3” #Welcome3”
Welcome has earned its place as a cult classic comedy. The film’s dialogues and scenes continue to be shared as memes on social media. 



Welcome to the Jungle has a star-studded ensemble cast including Suniel Shetty, Disha Patani, Raveena Tandon, Lara Dutta, Jacqueline Fernandez, Paresh Rawal, Arshad Warsi, Johnny Lever, Rajpal Yadav, Tusshar Kapoor, Shreyas Talpade, Krushna Abhishek, Kiku Sharda, Daler Mehndi, Mika Singh, Mukesh Tiwari, Zakir Hussain, Yashpal Sharma, and Sayaji Shinde.
The film will arrive in theatres on December 20, 2024. More on: Welcome to the Jungle, Akshay Kumar
