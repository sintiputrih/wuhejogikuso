TNA Impact Wrestling Spoiler Results for January 18, 2024

TNA Wrestling returned to Las Vegas on Sunday to tape their next set of television episodes for Impact Wrestling show on AXS. Here are the full results for the January 18, 2024 episode of those tapings:

Rhino defeated Shera.

Joe Hendry defeated Rich Swann.

In a multi-man match, Jake Something emerged victorious over KUSHIDA, El Hijo Del Vikingo, Trey Miguel, Laredo Kid, and Mike Bailey.

Nic Nemeth cut a promo about wanting to become the TNA World Champion before he was interrupted by Steve Maclin. Maclin called Nemeth a phony and claimed it was his time, as Nemeth had 20 years to tell his story. Nemeth ended the segment by hitting a Zig Zag on Maclin.

Xia Brookside defeated Tasha Steelz.

The Grizzled Young Veterans (James Drake & Zack Gibson) defeated Eric Young & Frankie Kazarian. After the match, Kazarian turned on Young.

PCO defeated Jai Vidal.

Chris Bey defeated Kevin Knight. Following the match, the Grizzled Young Veterans attacked Bey and Ace Austin.

Dirty Dango & Oleg Prudius defeated Dante King & Damian Drake.

Knockouts Champion Jordynne Grace retained her title by defeating Trinity. After the match, Gisele Shaw and her entourage attacked both Grace and Trinity.

Nic Nemeth defeated Zachary Wentz. After the match, Maclin ran out to beat down Nemeth, but Nemeth once again hit a Zig Zag on him.

MK Ultra (Killer Kelly & Masha Slamovich) defeated Jody Threat & Dani Luna.
