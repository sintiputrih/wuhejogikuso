PGA Tour 2k24 Release Date and 2k23 Trophy Guide

PGA Tour 2k24 release date is set for the second week of October 2023. The last Tiger Woods edition in 2022 was out on October 11, 2022.

Now, we might expect another game either in October this year (2023) or it could be in the next year depending on time for EA 

Based on the history of their release date, the previous games in the series have been released every two years, so PGA Tour 2K24 will likely be premiered in October 2024.

Making a fantastic golf game is challenging compared to games like Madden and NBA 2K because golf games need to be realistic and detailed.

Here are the release dates for the previous games in the series:

  • PGA Tour 2K23 – October 14, 2022
  • PGA Tour 2K21 – August 21, 2020

So, if the pattern continues, PGA Tour 2K24 could come out in 2024 fall.

PGA Tour 2k23 Locker Codes

Players can use locker codes in PGA 2k23 to get fantastic in-game items and virtual currency.

However, unlike other 2k games, such as NBA and AEW Fight Forever, they are not easily accessible. The game developers give out these codes on different platforms like Twitter, Discord, and Twitch live streams.

Here are some tips for finding locker codes:

  • Follow the PGA Tour 2K23 social media accounts (Twitter, Facebook, Instagram)
  • Check the official PGA Tour 2K23 website for new codes
  • Watch live streams of PGA Tour 2K23 tournaments
  • Join the PGA Tour 2K23 Discord server

If you keep an eye on these places, you might find some codes to redeem for gear or virtual currency (VC) in the game. Since the game’s release, the PGA Tour 2K Twitter account has shared many free codes for players on Xbox, PlayStation, and Steam.

The PGA Tour 2K subreddit revealed that the game developers have already distributed codes for in-game gear and VC to the players.

So, if you want some extra swag or goodies in the game, stay alert for those locker codes.

Besides, In February 2023, the official Twitter account of PGA Tour 2K posted a giveaway tweet asking, “Who wants a #PGATOUR2K23 code?”. They asked users to drop their consoles, and 2K DM’d lockers codes to them individually.

Furthermore, numerous individuals on Reddit and Discord are actively seeking these codes for the game. They have posted hundreds of inquiries on these platforms, hoping to uncover the game’s ciphers.

PGA 2k23 Course Designer

To design a course in PGA Tour 2K23 using the Course Designer mode, follow these steps:

  • Access the Course Designer – Launch PGA Tour 2K23 and navigate the Course Designer mode from the main menu.
  • Choose a Theme – Select one of the 12 available themes for your course. Themes include Autumn, Boreal, Countryside, Desert, Delta, Harvest, Tropical, Winter, Rustic, Steppe, Swiss and Highlands.
  • Customize Aesthetics – Use the available assets, such as trees, bushes, and other objects, to create the desired visual elements of your course.
  • Edit Layout – Design the layout of your golf course by placing tee boxes, fairways, bunkers, and greens.
  • Adjust Difficulty – Fine-tune the difficulty level of your course using the Course Designer’s tools. You can control the fairways’ length, the hazards’ placement, and the greens’ size and undulation to cater to players of different skill levels.
  • Use New Tools – Make use of the updated Course Designer tools, such as spline fences.
  • Enhance visuals – Utilize terrain, lighting, weather, and backdrop options.
  • Save and Upload – Once satisfied with your course design, save your creation. You also have the option to share it with the entire PGA Tour 2K23 community.

Is Augusta National On PGA 2k23?

Unfortunately, the Augusta National Golf Club of Georgia is not featured in PGA 2k23.

Augusta National is a private golf course and does not permit its representation to be included in video games.

In PGA Tour 2K23, even though Augusta National is not officially allowed to be featured, some community members have created courses inspired by it.

These user-made courses can be downloaded and played in the game, giving players a similar experience to the real Augusta National. However, they are not officially endorsed by the golf course itself.

Is PGA 2k23 Cross Platform?

Yes, PGA Tour 2K23 is a cross-platform game. That means PlayStation 4, PS 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S and PC players can play together online.

The 5th installment of the PGA Tour series supports cross-platform play so you can play your favorite PGA 2k23 multiplayer mode with your friends on PC and other consoles.

If you want to play with people on different platforms, first, you will need to set up a 2K account and connect it to your gaming account on your device. Once this is done, you can then encounter players from other platforms while playing online.

By following these steps, you can create or join a game with friends on multiple platforms:

  • Begin with Casual, Ranked, or TopGolf level
  • In the matchmaking menu, choose the option “Create Private Match” and press the right stick (R3/RS)
  • A code appears in the bottom right corner of the screen
  • Share that code with your friends
  • Then, select ‘Join With Room Code’, enter the Room Code your friend gave you, and hit ‘Confirm’.
