WWE Live Event Results From Kennewick, WA (9/16): Becky Lynch vs. Tiffany Stratton

On September 16, WWE held a WWE SuperShow live event from the Toyota Center Kennewick in Kennewick, WA. Among other matches, Becky Lynch faced Tiffany Stratton.

The results, courtesy of Wrestling Bodyslam, are as follows:

  • Rey Mysterio (c) def. LA Knight and Austin Theory
  •  Becky Lynch (c) def. Tiffany Stratton
  • Tommaso Ciampa & Alpha Academy (Chad Gable & Otis) (with Maxxine Dupri) def. Imperium (Gunther, Ludwig Kaiser & Giovanni Vinci)
  • Jey Uso def. Bronson Reed
  • Cody Rhodes def. Dominik Mysterio
  • The Judgment Day (Finn Balor & Damian Priest) (c) def. LWO (Cruz Del Toro & Joaquin Wilde)
  • Asuka def. Charlotte Flair
  • Seth Rollins (c) def. Shinsuke Nakamura
