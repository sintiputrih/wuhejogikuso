Replicas Of Alexa Bliss Doll Lilly Sell Out In Rapid Fashion

Alexa Bliss put her doll Lilly in timeout because of bad behavior. Odds are Lilly will be back because she is a hit with fans. WWE should smell money with Lilly, because that doll’s doll sold out in rapid fashion.

WWE started selling a Lilly plush doll. Fans had to act quickly because those things flew out of the online store at $30 a pop. Now they are sold out with no notice of when they will be replenished. Odds are WWE will restock this item so there’s no need to spend $100s on the secondary market yet.

The description for the item said it was hand-sown with a printed face. They also used skull beads for the doll’s teeth to add that extra bit of creepiness. The doll’s measurements are 17.5” h x 10.5” w and the brief description on the page told fans everything else they needed to know.

YOU TOO CAN HAVE A LILLY OF YOUR OWN. ALL YOU HAVE TO DO IS ASK NICELY!

Made of a super soft plush material, this doll will make the perfect gift for any member of the WWE Universe!

We’ll have to see if Lilly returns, but that doll could have money written all over her creepy face. Alexa Bliss is doing tricks like mirroring movements and other things that she can perform in front of live crowds. They are no longer in the ThunderDome, but that doesn’t mean Lilly has to remain gone for good.
