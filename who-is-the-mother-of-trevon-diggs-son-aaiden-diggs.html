Who Is The Mother Of Trevon Diggs Son Aaiden Diggs?

Trevon Diggs is an American football player who was born on September 20, 1997, in
Gaithersburg, Maryland. He was raised alongside his three siblings Darez, Stefon, and Avon
Diggs Jr. Sadly, Trevon’s father, Avon Diggs, died of congestive heart failure in January
2008 at the age of 39.

His brothers are also football players like him. Darez Diggs played for Friendship Collegiate
Academy Public Charter School in Washington, DC whereas Stefon Diggs played for Our
Lady of Good Counsel in Olney, Maryland. At present, Stefon plays for the Buffalo Bills as a
wide receiver.

Who Is The Mother Of Trevon Diggs’ Son Aaiden Diggs?
