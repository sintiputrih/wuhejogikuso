Don Wildman Biography

Don Wildman was born on March 21, 1961, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States of America. His personal life is largely unknown. We do know, though, that his father was a teacher and wished for Don to follow in his footsteps. Wildman claimed in an interview with the Travel Channel that three of his four sisters are or have been teachers at some point in their life.

Wildman was reared in Pitman, New Jersey, despite being born in Philadelphia. He went to Westtown Institution, which is a Quaker boarding school in Chester County, Pennsylvania. Quakers are members of a Protestant Christian denomination that dates back to the 16th century. In 1979, he graduated from Westtown School. He went on to Earlham College in Richmond, Indiana, and then to the Drama Studio in London, England, to study acting. Wildman began working as a server at the age of 16 in various restaurants to supplement his income. Working as a server, he later said, taught him a lot about life.

Wildman sets out to investigate and discover some of the world’s biggest secrets in Weird Travels. It addressed a wide spectrum of subjects, from the supernatural to the outlandish.
