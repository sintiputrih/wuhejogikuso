Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie Reportedly Put Their Divorce On Hold

Could Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt be rethinking their divorce? According to a new report from Us Weekly, the former couple may be considering a reconciliation in the future.

The source revealed, "The divorce is off. They haven’t done anything to move it forward in several months and no one thinks they are ever going to.” Now that Pitt has quit drinking and started working on himself, Jolie has reportedly had a change of heart in her decision to file for divorce after the alleged plane ride fight took place nearly a year ago.

"He got sober to try and win her back. He knew he had a problem that he had to take care of. And that’s all she ever wanted," the source added.

Getty Images

A reported "friend of Jolie's" also commented that the actress would consider taking Pitt back if "he showed he was committed to raising a family." Since Jolie filed for divorce nearly a year ago, Pitt and Jolie have reportedly been attending family therapy sessions with their six children along with individual sessions.

In his recent cover story with GQ Style, Pitt opened up about coping after divorce and becoming a better father for his children: "I grew up with a Father-knows-best/war mentality—the father is all-powerful, super strong—instead of really knowing the man and his own self-doubt and struggles. And it's hit me smack in the face with our divorce: I gotta be more. I gotta be more for them. I have to show them. And I haven't been great at it."

Neither Pitt or Jolie have commented on the reconciliation rumors, but chances are they'll keep things private for the sake of their children.
