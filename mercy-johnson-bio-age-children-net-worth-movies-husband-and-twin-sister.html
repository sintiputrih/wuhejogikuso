Mercy Johnson Biography

Mercy Johnson born as Mercy Johnson Okojie is a Nigerian actress. Mercy is a popular motivational speaker. Mercy made her acting debut in the movie, “The Maid” playing the role of house help. Her performance in the movie shot her into the limelight and she has acted in other major movies ever since.

Mercy attended Nigerian Navy primary school, Ojo, and then moved to Nigerian navy secondary school Bonkiri port-Harcourt. Thereafter, Mercy gained a degree in computer science at then NVC Port-Harcourt. After a while, she got admission to study the English language at the Lagos State University but after her 2nd year, she deferred due to a financial constraint.

Mercy Johnson Age

Mercy is 35 years as of 2019, she was born on 28 August 1984 in Lagos, Nigeria. Mercy celebrates her birthday on August 28th every year. She will be turning 36 years as of August 28, 2020.

Mercy Johnson Family

Mercy comes from a very large family of seven children. Daniel Johnson, a military man, and Elizabeth Johnson. Her mother Elizabeth Johnson, passed on in May 2018. Mercy follows three older sisters. Additionally, she has three younger brothers.

Her elder sisters taught her to take care of her family and do house chores. Whereas, her brothers made her a tomboy who enjoyed games and activities that are traditionally designed for boys. Her siblings are not public figures and they stay away from the spotlight and the actress does her best to protect their privacy.

Mercy Johnson Husband

Mercy tied the knot with her husband Prince Odianosen Okojie, popularly known as Odi in 2011. Their wedding ceremony was held at Christ Embassy, Oregun, Ikeja.

Traditional marriage was held on 26th August at Iyana Iba Lagos. It was a grand occasion attended by many dignitaries including Nollywood stars and top government officials.
