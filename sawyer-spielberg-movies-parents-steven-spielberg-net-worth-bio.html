Sawyer Spielberg To Make Feature Film Debut With 2020 Horror &quot;Honeydew&quot;

Growing up in a reputed family as the son of a legendary director and a veteran actress, upcoming actor Sawyer Spielberg must have felt quite a pressure to maintain his family's legacy.

His father Steven Spielberg is known for directing multiple critically and commercially successful movies like Jurassic Park (1993), Schindler's List (1993), Saving Private Ryan (1998), Catch Me If You Can (2002), Lincoln (2012), the Indiana Jones franchise, and more.

Meanwhile, Sawyer's mother, Kate Capshaw, is famous for her acting performance in films such as Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom (1984), Love Affair (1994), The Alarmist (1997), A Girl Thing (2001), and so on.

Following his parents' footsteps, Sawyer also joined the film industry but not immediately as a leading actor. He made his way up from the bottom and is soon to make his feature film debut.

Sawyer Is Set To Make Debut Via 2020 Horror Movie, Career Info

Sawyer started his career through his father's directorial movie- Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull (2008). However, he was not a part of the cast; instead, he acted as a production assistant.

Later, he acted in a couple of short movies: Afterglow (III) (2012) & Breathe (2016), and he even made a small appearance in another one of his father's directed movie- The Post (2017).

Despite working in a few movies and short films, Sawyer is yet to make his silver screen debut as a lead actor. However, the wait won't be long as the young Spielberg is yet to make his feature film debut and not in one of his dad's movies.

Sawyer's debut film Honeydew- which will premiere at Berlin's European Film Market in late-February 2020- has been labeled a "feverish horror" and is based in New England.

Sawyer Spielberg is set to make his feature film debut with the 2020 horror movie, Honeydew (©: HorrorFuel)

In the upcoming film, Sawyer has worked together with director Devereux Milburn and several talented cast members, including Malin Barr, Barbara Kingsley, Stephen D'Ambrose, Jamie Bradley, and more.

The movie revolves around a couple who develop strange yearnings and delusions after taking shelter at the home of an old farmer and her son.

Sawyer Spielberg's Bio: Parents, Family, Siblings & More

A native of Los Angeles, California, Sawyer Spielberg was born as the youngest son of Steven Spielberg and Kate Capshaw on 10 March 1992 (age: 27).

The young actor comes from a large family with seven siblings. Out of his seven brothers and sisters, Sasha and Destry are Sawyer's biological siblings; whereas, Jessica Capshaw and Max Spielberg are half-siblings from his parents' former relationships.

Sawyer Spielberg (right) with parents: Steven Spielberg, Kate Capshaw, and siblings (©: CloserWeekly)

Likewise, Theo and Mikaela Spielberg are adopted children of Sawyer's parents.

Sawyer And Parents' Net Worth Comparison

Though it may be too soon in Sawyer's career to have a verified net worth, the same doesn't apply to his parents. His father, Steven Spielberg, enjoys his status as a billionaire movie director with a net worth of $3.7 billion.

Similarly, Sawyer's mother, Kate Capshaw's net worth, may be nowhere near her husband's; she still possesses a net worth of $20 million- which is a significant amount compared to other actresses.
