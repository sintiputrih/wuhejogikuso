Roy Black Net Worth


The net worth of Leroy Black is estimated to be $65 million. He is a well-known criminal and civil defense trial attorney, widely recognized for representing prominent figures like Rush Limbaugh and securing an acquittal for William Kennedy Smith on charges of rape. Born on 17 February 1945 in New York City, USA, his successful career and high-profile cases have notably contributed to his wealth.

Roy Black’s parents divorced shortly after his birth, and he was raised by his mother, who remarried in 1951. His family moved to Connecticut and then Jamaica before returning to the US, where he pursued higher education at the University of Miami. After graduating in 1967, he went on to study at the University Of Miami School Of Law, where he earned a Juris Doctor and achieved the highest possible score on the Florida Bar Exam.

As a defense attorney, Black has handled numerous high-profile cases, further solidifying his reputation and financial success. He is currently a partner at Black, Srebnick, Kornspan, & Stumpf – a Miami-based trial firm specializing in criminal defense and civil litigation. Known for his eloquent courtroom arguments and dedication to his clients, he has expanded his influence through regular appearances on television as a legal commentator for news programs on NBC.

In addition to his legal career, Black has been involved in reality television, serving as the managing partner in the reality competition series “The Law Firm”. He has also made appearances on “The Real Housewives of Miami” alongside his third wife, Lisa Lea Haller, adding to his public profile and financial success.

Despite three marriages, Roy Black prefers to keep his personal life relatively private. His second marriage to Naomi Morris Black in 1984 resulted in a daughter, and in 1994, he married Lisa Lea Haller, with whom he has a son.

Apart from his professional and personal endeavors, Roy Black has been involved in television and film, with notable appearances in “The Law Firm” as a host and various TV series such as “A Crime to Remember”, “Good Morning America”, and “The Early Show”.

With an estimated net worth of $65 million, Roy Black’s career accomplishments and public engagements have significantly contributed to his financial status.
