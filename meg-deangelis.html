Meg DeAngelis - Bio, Net Worth, Single, Nationality, Career, Facts

Table of Biography

Meg DeAngelis is a popular Canadian YouTube star known for her channel ‘MayBaby’. Meg DeAngelis shares her life with a series of cheer routines and outfit of the day videos. Moreover, she also covers health, fitness, and beauty in her videos.

Early Life and Childhood

Meg DeAngelis turns 28 years old in 2024. Her full name is Megan Nicole DeAngelis. She was born on May 15th, 1995, in Toronto, Canada.

Her birth sign is Taurus and she is Canadian by nationality. Although she was born in Canada, she later moved to Germany with her engineer’s parents. Moreover, she has a twin sister named Ashley.

She attended the University of Central Florida, where she joined the cheerleading squad. She was once interested in business marketing though now she is more inclined toward film studies. Likewise, she is a former junior gymnastics teacher.

Professional Life

Meg’s first video was of her doing a back handspring for cheerleading, having been a member of the University of Central Florida cheerleading team at one point.

After that, she got a lot of positive responses and popularity. Initially, she only did it for herself. While she watched other tutorials online, she never actually thought that people would watch hers, which were more of a means of getting better at gymnastics.

Furthermore, she starred in a YouTube Red original movie called ‘Dance Camp’. She’s also part of the sketch comedy group ‘just like that. She is one of the voice actors in the animated film ‘Trolls’.

She also has a role in the movies, ‘Millennials: The Musical’, and ‘Royal Crush‘. In 2015, she made her theatrical film debut as April in the musical film Summer Forever wherein she acted alongside actresses Megan Nicole, Alyson Stoner, and Anna Grace Barlow.

She offered a few anecdotes about her life in videos, almost as an aside, like mentioning that she’d just come home from school before she started rolling, making a quick comment on how she looked, and more.

However, after visiting a friend in Los Angeles and discovering the YouTube community there, she dropped out of college and moved across the country to try and make it as a professional YouTuber. She featured Cody Simpson in a Q&A in one of her videos.

Moreover, she is also signed with the multi-channel network Awesomeness TV, and, like other lifestyle YouTubers before her, she has started designing for an AwesomenessTV clothing line.

She gets paid through Google AdSense and makes enough money from YouTube to pay her rent and fund her productions.

Not just fashion, she has now branched out to DIY videos which have gained immense popularity, especially among girls because of how simple the projects are.
