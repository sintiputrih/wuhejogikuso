Perrie Edwards Wiki, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Biography, Family &amp; More

Perrie Edwards (born July 10, 1993) is a British Singer from South Shields, Tyne & Wear, England. She is famous for being a member of the ace girl group named “Little Mix“. In 2011, Perrie participated in the 8th season of X Factor UK (Singing Reality Show). Furthermore, the 25-year-old singer formed the group during the show.

Little Mix group also came out as a winner of the show. In fact, it was the first group that ever won the reality series. Other members of the band are Jade Thirlwall, Leigh-Anne Pinnock, and Jesy Nelson.

In addition, the music group released their debut album titled “DNA” in 2012. The album also reached on the top 5 spots on the US and UK charts. Moreover, it also gained the highest debut US chart position for a British girl group and also defeated “Spice Girls”.

In 2013, Little Mix released their second album “Salute” which was also a hit. The group’s third album “Get Weird” released in 2015. Furthermore, their 4th album “Glory Days” (2016) debuted on the first position on various charts in the UK.

Some of the best single tracks of the band are “Wings”, “Black Magic” and “Shout Out to My Ex”. In fact, Little Mix won serval awards includes two MTV Europe Music Awards, two Teen Choice Awards, and two Glamour Awards.

Profile, Parents & Before Fame

The singer was born on the 10th of July 1993 in South Shields, Tyne & Wear, England, UK. Presently, Perrie Edwards’ age is 25 years old. Her parents are Alexander Edwards (father) and Deborah Duffy (mother).

Furthermore, the star singer has an older brother named Jonnie, and a younger half-sister named Caitlin Edwards. Her half-sibling is from her father’s side. Edwards attended Radipole Primary School in Weymouth, Dorset.

Later, she moved to St. Peter Primary School and then Paul RC Primary School. Perrie later enrolled in the Mortimer Community College. The singer transferred and graduated from Newcastle College. She received a BTEC in Performing Arts.

Personal Affairs, Boyfriend & Spouse

Perrie started her relationship with boyfriend Zayn Malik (UK singer and former One Direction member) in May 2012. The couple also engaged each other in August 2013. However, they broke up in August 2015 due to some personal reasons. Zayn later dated Gigi Hadid (professional model) but we are not sure that if they are still together or not.

Currently, she is dating English professional footballer Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain. The love partner confirmed their affair in February 2017. They also upload their cute pictures together on their social media accounts.
