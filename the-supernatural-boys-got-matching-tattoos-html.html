Jeffrey Dean Morgan, Jared Padalecki, and Jensen Ackles Got Matching Tattoos

Supernatural, the CW’s spooky juggernaut of brotherly love, is ending this season and people are getting emotional. This past weekend was Jeffrey Dean Morgan’s wedding to Hilarie Burton. And what better way to celebrate your lifelong bond with your wife than matching tattoos with your fictional sons? The Winchester boys all got matching tattoos at Morgan’s wedding, to celebrate their decade and a half as fake family. “@jaredpadalecki @jensenackles and myself will forever have a bond that is both special, and permanent. Love you both dearly,” Morgan wrote on Instagram. Padalecki commented “Family who slays together, stays together.” In classic LOTR cast style, the Winchester boys aren’t revealing exactly what the tattoo is, but here’s hoping it’s a Fanfiction.net disclaimer, in honor of the fans.
