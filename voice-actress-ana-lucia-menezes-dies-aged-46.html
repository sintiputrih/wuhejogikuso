Voice Actress Ana Lcia Menezes Dies Aged 46

The Pernambuco Ana Lúcia Menezes, known for her voice acting in several successful series, passed away on Tuesday (20), after complications caused by a stroke that occurred last Friday (17). The information was released by her daughter, Bia Menezes, in a publication on social networks. Bia is also a voice actor and wrote on Instagram that she will continue her mother’s legacy.

“You wrote a story here, and I have an obligation to continue it because I know it would be exactly as you would like me to do. You fought until the last minute, now it’s time to rest and enjoy what God has prepared for you”, commented Bia.

With over 30 years of career, the voice of Ana Lúcia is present in works such as Tonya, in Everybody hates Chris; Sam Pucket, in iCarly ; Ahsoka Tano, in Star Wars: The Clone Wars; Lupita, in Rebelde and Suzy, in Peppa Pig.
