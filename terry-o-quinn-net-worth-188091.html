Terry O'Quinn Net Worth

Terry O'Quinn is an American actor born on July 15, 1952 in Sault Ste. Marie, MI. He is best known for his role as John Locke on the hit TV series Lost. He has also had recurring roles in Hawaii Five-0 and 666 Park Avenue.

About
