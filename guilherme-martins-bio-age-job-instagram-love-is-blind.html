Guilherme Martins Job

Guilherme Martins is an Air Traffic Controller by profession. He has been working at Infraero Brazil since February 2016 and has 5 years of experience at Vitoria-ES Airport.

Some of his responsibilities as an Air Traffic Controller at Vitoria-ES airport include providing safe, orderly, and dynamic air traffic; controlling the flow of aircraft in the airspace under the Control Tower’s jurisdiction using radio equipment, computational equipment, and visual references; authorizing landings and takeoffs of aircraft on competing runways, provide meteorological and navigation information for pilots in flight, and others.

On October 19, 2018, Guilherme shared throwback videos to his first Vix-SP cabin flight on Instagram.

Prior to working at Infraero Brasil, he was an Expansion and Sales Analyst at Multivix, where he worked from October 2020 to June 2021. There he had to sell distance learning Poles concessions to investors, implement CRM Scaling in the Commercial expansion sector, create controls, and carry out training.

From August 2015 to December 2015, Guilherme worked as a restaurant worker at North Dakota State University. There he served students in the cafeteria and prepare drinks, assisted in cleaning and organizing the kitchen, prepared and served food, and took care of the cleaning and organization of the common environment of the restaurant.

Some of Guilherme’s other past jobs include being co-founder of Clube START (January 2014 – August 2015), an initiative focused on entrepreneurial education and networking; co-founder of Teaching Group at Luma School (March 2014 – March 2015), an initiative focused on education with the main objective of democratizing teaching for students with difficulties in specific subjects of primary, secondary and higher education; and Consultant at Chico Bento Restaurant (May 2013 – September 2013).

Talking about Guilherme’s education, he received his Bachelor’s Degree in Industrial Engineering in 2015. Next year, he joined ICEA-Air Traffic Control Institute to become the Air Traffic Controller of Aviation/Airway Management and Operations. Furthermore, in 2021, he received his Engineer’s Degree in Production Engineering from the Federal University of Espírito Santo.
