Meet Dewayne Bryan and Annette DeAnn (Mullen) Bryan

American singer-songwriter Zach Bryan has amassed a sizable following in the folk and country music genres.

Zach Bryan, who was born on March 6, 1996, in Tulsa, Oklahoma, has swiftly become well-known for his passionate and reflective songs, enthralling voice, and raw, genuine approach that appeals to a broad audience.

The honesty of Zach Bryan’s music is one of its most distinctive features. In order to write songs that seem real and relevant, he frequently draws on his own experiences and feelings.

Fans who value his abilities to tell intricate stories and convey complicated emotions through his lyrics have taken a liking to him because of his sincerity.

His songs address issues like love, loss, self-discovery, and the difficulties of daily existence.

Meet Dewayne Bryan and Annette DeAnn (Mullen) Bryan

Although Bryan was raised in Oologah, Oklahoma, his family was stationed overseas with the Navy when he was born in Okinawa, Japan.

Zach Bryan has a sister named Mackenzie and is the son of Dewayne Bryan and Annette DeAnn (Mullen) Bryan.

What is Zach Bryan’s background?

Bryan was born in Okinawa, Japan, on April 2, 1996, when his parents were stationed there. He later grew up in Oologah, Oklahoma.

Both of Bryan’s parents, as well as his grandparents, uncles, and great-grandfather, all served in the U.S. Navy, according to The Oklahoman.

Where is Zach Bryan from?

A singer/songwriter from Oologah, Oklahoma, Zach Bryan is motivated to stay true to himself and is proud of his small-town background.

The 25-year-old was honourably discharged from the Navy after serving for seven years.
